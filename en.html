Who am I?

Hello! My name is Marcello Miranda and I'm 12 years old. I am the owner of Rena, an organization that includes several websites and web apps that I have developed entirely by myself, with lots of advice from my team. I'm proud to present to you my projects: Rena Store, Rena Arcades, and Rena Developer, which we'll discuss in more detail later.

The story of Rena

Rena was born in 2020, when I was just 8 years old. Initially, I focused on creating concept videos, experimenting with creative ideas, and learning the basics of digital production. A few years later, in 2022, I started building websites using no-code tools, which allowed me to turn my ideas into reality without needing to write code. Then, in 2023, by learning HTML, CSS, and JavaScript, I redesigned all the no-code websites into HTML and hosted them on Altervista.
